# Andrea Rebucini

I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Bergamo. My research lies in applied microeconomics, with particular interests in financial literacy, household economics, economics of education, gender economics, health economics, labour economics, and impact evaluation.

I am currently affiliated with the Department of Economics at the University of Bergamo. In 2025–2026, I am visiting the Department of Social Policy at the London School of Economics and Political Science, where I am affiliated with the Women in Social and Public Policy Research Hub.
