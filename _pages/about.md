# Andrea Rebucini

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of Bergamo, where I am affiliated with the Department of Economics and supervised by Prof. Annalisa Cristini. I hold an MSc in Economics and Data Analysis and a BSc in Economics from the University of Bergamo. I spent the academic year 2025–26 as a visiting research student at the Department of Social Policy of the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE), where I was affiliated with the Women in Social and Public Policy Research Hub (WISPPRH), hosted by Prof. Almudena Sevilla.  
My research interests lie in applied microeconomics, economics of education, financial literacy, gender economics, health economics, and household economics.   
If you want to get in touch, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
